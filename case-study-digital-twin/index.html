About the project

Client Overview

A rapidly expanding company with a large portfolio of physical spaces across multiple countries.

Problem Statement

The client needed to monitor and optimize their built environments at scale. They struggled with deploying and managing diverse IoT devices, lacked spatial context for sensor data, and had difficulty integrating various building management systems.

Objectives

  1. Monitor physical spaces and interactions between people and the environment
  2. Improve workplace efficiency and employee engagement
  3. Optimize space utilization and reduce operational costs
  4. Enhance environmental conditions in built spaces
Solution Overview

We developed a comprehensive IoT platform that enabled deployment, management, and integration of connected devices within the built environment, effectively creating a Digital Twin of the physical spaces.

Hardware Solution

Our hardware approach combined custom sensors for real-time data transmission with retrofitted off-the-shelf devices. We implemented a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) over-the-air (OTA) firmware update mechanism, allowing for remote maintenance of devices in hard-to-reach locations.

Software Solution

The software ecosystem comprised a device management platform, a data processing system, and user applications. We developed a unified platform that integrated diverse IoT devices and building systems, along with APIs for occupancy and environmental data insights. Our solution included deployment planning tools, a mobile app for installers, and real-time data processing capabilities with spatial context enrichment.

Key Features

BLE Over The Air (OtA) firmware update mechanism.

Scalable device deployment and management system.

Spatial context integration for data enrichment.

Unified platform for diverse IoT devices and building systems.

Real-time processing of sensor data with edge/cloud capabilities.

Conclusion

This project showcased innovative approaches to large-scale IoT device management and spatial data integration, applicable across various industries requiring extensive IoT deployments. By creating a Digital Twin of the physical spaces, we empowered the client to make data-driven decisions about their real estate portfolio, significantly improving efficiency, reducing costs, and enhancing user experience across their global operations. This solution demonstrates the power of unifying diverse systems and data sources to create a comprehensive, actionable view of physical spaces.
